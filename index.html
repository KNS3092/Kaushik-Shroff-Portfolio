About Me

I’m a graduate student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, with a strong interest in computer architecture and hardware design. My fascination with computers started when I built my first PC in high school, and that hands-on experience led me to pursue engineering. Over time, I’ve worked on various projects involving hardware systems, embedded design, and digital electronics, always looking for ways to improve efficiency and performance. I enjoy problem-solving, working with technology, and collaborating on new ideas. Outside of academics, I like building computers, exploring new hardware, and keeping up with the latest in tech.
